who we are

born from one
simple belief.

Mutual Friends was born from one simple belief: no one should feel like a stranger.

Based in Jakarta, we create offline events and an online community where people meet, share, and turn moments into lasting friendships. No niche, no filter — just intentional design that makes connection feel natural.

Our events aren’t just social gatherings. They’re curated experiences where brands live authentically inside the moment — not on top of it.

“we design genuine human moments in Jakarta where anyone can walk in alone and leave feeling like they belong — and brands live inside that, not on top of it.”
